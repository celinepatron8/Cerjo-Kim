How it began

Our Story

Cerjo and Kim first met during their college years. Back then, Cerjo tried to make Kim feel that he liked her, but things didn't quite fall into place. Life went on, and their paths drifted apart.

Eight years later, in 2021, fate brought them back together. It all started with a simple comment Cerjo left on one of Kim's social media stories. From that small moment, something beautiful began to grow.

What once was a missed chance turned into a second beginning — one that led them to where they are now.

And today, their story continues… on a journey toward forever. 💍✨

Frequently Asked Questions

As love is what this day is all about, your presence is one we couldn't celebrate without. However, should you insist that a gift is worth giving, a small saving for our future is a delightful blessing.

Please refer to the number of seat/s allotted for you. As much as we want to celebrate with everybody, unfortunately we can only accommodate a limited number of guests due to venue and budget restrictions.

Children are really adorable but our event is an adults-only event. Only children who are part of the entourage are included in both the ceremony and reception.

We have assigned seats for each one of you. It took us a lot of effort and discussion to finalize the seating arrangement, which is meant for everyone's convenience and group familiarity — so no need to worry. Our coordinators will gladly assist you in finding your designated seats.

NO RSVP = NO SEAT. We will assign your seats so we strongly suggest you do the RSVP on or before May 25, 2026, or you may contact and inform us ahead of time. In any case that you may have said Yes in our RSVP but suddenly won't be able to attend, please let us know ASAP so we can accommodate any changes.

The ceremony will strictly start at 4:00 PM. Entourage will march by 3:30 PM. Please be at the venue at least 10–15 minutes before 3:30 PM.

The program is estimated to last until 9:00 PM. We are hoping that you will stay with us until the end of the program. Should you wish to leave early, we'd love to personally thank you and bid goodbye before you go — perhaps not until the same-day video is played.
